WELCOME TO MAGS FOUNDATION

about us

Mags Foundation is a non-profit organisation.A visionary who has heart for the orphans,the sick and prisoner. Restoring hope to those who were hopeless. We work with orphans children by providing a safe enviroment for them. We provide school fees and food for all the children. We work with woman that give birth and have no hosipital fees. We provide food and babyclothes for them. We work with minar who are in prision by providing councilling session for rehabitation. We also provide traning course for them where they can read and write. we also provide mentorship to guide them for bright future. Here in mags foundation we beleive everyone deserved a life

One Vision

We beleive all everyone deserved a life. In DR Congo the percentage of orphans is very high. Our vision is to provide a safe enviroment for all the children where they can call home. We want to give the children joyce and happy life. We want to restore hopes that their life is precious and they have bright future

One Passion

Mags Foundation passion is helping hopeless and abanded child by showing them love and support. Every time we remove one child from the stree and provide a warm shatter for them it gives us happiness

One Vocation

This is a called for us, we believe were put in this world to help one another.We don't take this as a job or hobbies this is a vocation for us. This is a must duties that we must accomplish.
